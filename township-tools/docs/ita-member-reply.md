# Email template — reply to an ITA member asking about Township Tools access

Use this when an ITA member emails wondering whether their ITA membership
includes access to the paid tools on Township Tools. Personalize the greeting
and any context-specific notes before sending.

---

**Subject:** Quick note about Township Tools and your ITA membership

Hi [Name],

Thanks so much for reaching out, and thank you for being an ITA member!

I want to gently clarify how this site works, because it actually hosts two
different things — and I think the distinction will help answer your question:

**Free, as part of your ITA membership — no login required:**
- **SB 270 Scoring Tool** — just open it, no sign-up or account needed.
- **Contact Verification Portal** — same: just open the link, no account needed.

If you came looking for either of those, you're already covered — just head
straight to the tool from the homepage and you're set.

**A friendly add-on — separate, optional service (not included in ITA dues):**
Everything else on the site is a private service I run separately. It's here
to help townships that want a little extra help with the day-to-day work, like
building annual reports faster or sending nicer newsletters. **It isn't a
replacement for what ITA already provides — just a friendly add-on if you
find it useful.**

You may recognize my name because I'm also separately contracted by ITA to
manage their official website and provide tech support — that's how I came to
build the two free tools above. The paid tools, however, are my own work,
offered to townships on a subscription.

**Simple pricing:**
- **Subscription — $10/month or $96/year per township (annual saves 20%).**
  Covers the **Report Builder** and **Email Template Builder**. No long
  contracts — cancel anytime. About the price of a cup of coffee a week.
- **Report Design Service — custom quote.** Send your materials and I'll
  design your annual report. Price depends on the number of sections and the
  amount of content. No subscription required — pay per project.
- **Custom-built tools — from $150 per tool.** Need something built just for
  your township (a calculator, data tracker, custom template)? Priced by
  scope, one-time build fee, plus a 10% annual maintenance fee starting year
  2 to keep your tool running. Email me to discuss.

If any of that would be useful, just reply to this email and I'll help you get
set up — no pressure either way.

In the meantime, the free tools are always available to you as an ITA member.
Please continue to reach out to ITA staff for anything else tied to your
membership.

Thanks again for being in touch — happy to help either way!
